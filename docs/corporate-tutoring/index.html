At Beyond Tutors, we recognize that language proficiency varies among professionals. That's why our corporate tutoring services cater to individuals at all levels – from those who are just starting their English language journey to those aiming for advanced fluency.

Our corporate tutoring program focuses on English usage in professional settings. We cover a wide range of skills necessary for success in the business world, including:

- Business Negotiations: Learn how to navigate complex negotiations confidently and effectively.
- Telephone Conversations: Develop clear and concise telephone communication skills for seamless business interactions.
- Written Reports and Emails: Master the art of writing professional reports, emails, and other written communication.
- Professional Presentations: Sharpen your presentation skills to leave a lasting impression in board meetings and conferences.

At Beyond Tutors, we believe in learning from the best. Our tutors are carefully selected from the finest institutions across the United States and the United Kingdom, ensuring that you receive the highest quality education available. We are proud of our partnership with the University of Cambridge, a testament to our commitment to excellence.

Our tutors are not just educators; they are mentors who will work closely with you to achieve your language learning goals. With their expertise and guidance, you can be confident in your journey towards English language proficiency.

Getting started with Beyond Tutors's corporate tutoring is easy. Our streamlined process ensures that you are matched with the right tutor for your needs.

Here's how it works:

- Initial Consultation: We begin with an initial consultation to understand your company's goals, language proficiency level, and scheduling preferences.
- Tutor Matching: Our team will carefully match your employees with a tutor who best aligns with your objectives and availability. We also offer group tutoring.
- Learning Plan: Your tutor will develop a customized learning plan tailored to your specific needs and goals.
- Flexible Scheduling: We understand that professionals have busy schedules. That's why our tutoring sessions are designed to fit around your working hours.

- Group Tuition: Starting from as little as £20 per hour per person, our group tutoring sessions offer a cost-effective way to improve your English language skills in a supportive environment.

- Private 1:1 Tuition: For personalized attention and a customized learning experience, our private 1:1 tutoring sessions are available from £59 per hour.

Invest in your professional development and communication skills with Beyond Tutors's corporate tutoring services. Contact us today to begin your journey towards English language excellence in the corporate world.

Business English is an umbrella term to describe any language skills that are used in a professional environment. At the most basic, this could be sending and receiving emails written in English and being able to clearly present your ideas. As you become more proficient in this skill, your business English tuition may then focus on speaking and listening skills, giving presentations, and conducting negotiations. <br> <br> At its core, business English tuition is about instilling confidence in an ESL speaker, helping them to build fluency, and streamlining their journey toward success Flourishing in the corporate environment requires a great understanding of English, which our world-class business English 1:1 sessions can offer. <br> <br> Most of all, business English is an investment for life. Whether you’re only preparing for an interview or are looking to break into the English-speaking job market, these vital skills will stay with you over the course of your career.

Absolutely anyone that speaks English as a second language can take a business English course. Whether your English is already at a conversational level or needs slightly more work, Beyond Tutors offers English tuition for all. <br> <br> If you’re actively working in an international company that deals with English-speaking clients, or you want to work in this world, then business English is often a mandatory requirement. With Beyond Tutors' personalised business English courses, you’ll be well on your way to improving and securing more career opportunities in your future.

For those learning English as a second language (ESL), business English may seem like an oddly specific area to focus on. However, in the day-to-day world, the language, vocabulary, and structures used in formal English are vastly different to those used in everyday conversation. <br> <br> By focusing on improving your level of business English, you’re able to drive forward communication, creating strong business partnerships internationally. With over 1.4 billion native or ESL speakers, investing in your English education is an investment into the potential of your own career. From securing a job in an English-speaking company to striking up international business connections, business English tuition can help you succeed.

As an international language, the very best business English tutors are all available online. Instead of having to commute or schedule lessons at an awkward time, our team of online tutors allows you to fit your education into your own schedule. Instead of a tutor near you, you’ll be able to turn to our online platform to find the very best tutor for you. <br> <br> Our base of online tutors are accessible around the clock, meaning you can find an expert whenever suits you.

At Beyond Tutors, we understand that you may conduct much more business within certain territories. With this in mind, we’ve cultivated a team of expert tutors that specialise in UK English or US English. No matter what accent or style you want to focus on, we’ll be able to find the perfect tutor for you.
