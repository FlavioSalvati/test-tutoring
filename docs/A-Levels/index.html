Selecting the right path for your child's A-Level education is a pivotal decision that profoundly impacts their university trajectory and future career opportunities.

At Beyond Tutors, we understand the importance of this choice. That's why we offer specialized advisory services that help you navigate through this defining period.

Our holistic approach begins by helping you select, and gain entrance to, the top A-Level schools for your child. We consider factors like subject choices, extracurricular pursuits, competitions (e.g., olympiads), projects and summer internships; all to enrich their academic portfolio.

Drawing upon our extensive knowledge and connections within top schools and universities, we provide personalized recommendations that set your child on the path to academic excellence and a successful career.

Embarking on A-Level courses can be overwhelming. At Beyond Tutors, we believe in building confidence from the very beginning.

Our A-Level Foundation Programme introduces key topics in each subject ahead of the start of A-Level. This is usually done in the summer after Year 12, which marks the start of A-Level. Our 3-month Online & In-Person course relieves stress associated with the transition into A-Level, instilling confidence and enthusiasm in your child.

Our programme delves into the chosen A-Level subjects and covers all the prerequesites as well as topics from the AS syllabus. Tuition is molded around your child, bringing them as far into the syllabus as we can.

The A-Level Foundation Programme can be charged on an hourly-basis or as a discounted package. The cost will depend on the number of subjects and weekly sessions.

With Beyond Tutors's A-Level Foundation Programme, your child embarks on their A-Level journey with a solid foundation, making the academic year ahead a rewarding and enriching experience.

Our tutors work closely with your child to identify their strengths and weaknesses, tailoring lessons to their needs. Our tutors are also trained to identify and address any gaps in your child's knowledge, ensuring they're up to speed with the curriculum.

With our weekly tuition sessions, your child receives dedicated attention from our experienced tutors. Outside lessons, our tutors meticulously craft assignments that reinforce your child's understanding and mastery of each A-Levels subject. Monthly progress reports keep you informed about your child's achievements and areas for improvement.

With Beyond Tutors's year-round tuition, your child receives unwavering guidance and resources to excel academically in A-Levels and beyond.

Closer to exams, Beyond Tutors provides targeted exam preparation. We work to pair you with a skilled tutor within 24 hours, close to exam season.

If you're enrolled in our year-round tuition, your child will begin targeted exam preparation around this period with their regular tutor, who will be familiar with their strengths and weaknesses. Close to exams, we recommend daily lessons to maximise preparation. Personalized revision assignments keep your child engaged between lessons. We provide weekly progress reports to ensure you're kept in the loop about your child's progress in this sensitive time.

With Beyond Tutors's targeted exam preparation, your child can approach their exams with confidence, armed with the skills and knowledge they need to obtain the grades they need.

A-Levels are two-year courses taken by students in the UK after completing their GCSEs. A-Levels are typically chosen by students aged 16-19 and are highly regarded by universities and employers as they demonstrate a high level of academic ability and independent learning. <br> <br> There is a wide range of subjects that students can choose to study at A-Level, including English, Maths, Sciences, Humanities, and Languages. Each subject has a specific syllabus which outlines what topics will be covered over the two-year period.<br> <br> A-Levels are assessed by exams taken at the end of the second year of study, usually in May or June. These exams are designed to test students' knowledge and understanding of the subject matter, as well as their ability to apply this knowledge to real-world situations.<br> <br> Students take A-Levels for various reasons, including to gain entry to university or to progress in their chosen career. A-Levels provide students with a broad and in-depth understanding of their chosen subjects, helping them to develop a range of transferable skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, and analytical skills.

A tutor can provide valuable assistance to students studying for A-Levels. Tutors are highly experienced and knowledgeable professionals who can help students to understand complex concepts, identify their strengths and weaknesses, and develop effective study strategies. They can offer individualised attention and support, helping students to address their specific needs and challenges. <br> <br> One of the most significant ways that a tutor can help students study for A-Levels is by providing personalised guidance and feedback. Tutors can assess the student's understanding of the subject and provide feedback on their performance. They can also tailor their teaching methods to the student's learning style and pace, ensuring that the student is making the most of their study time. <br> <br> In addition to subject-specific assistance, tutors can also help students to develop essential study skills such as time management, note-taking, and exam preparation. By working with a tutor, students can gain confidence and build the skills and knowledge they need to excel in their A-Level exams.

Finding the best A-Level tutor for you involves several key steps. First, consider your individual learning style and the areas in which you need the most help. Look for tutors who have experience working with students at your level and who are familiar with the exam board you are studying under. <br> <br> At Beyond Tutors, we make it easy to find the best A-Level tutor for you. Our academic consultants take the time to understand your needs and learning style, then match you with an A-Level tutor who can help you achieve your goals. Our experienced tutors have a deep understanding of the A-Level curriculum and can provide tailored support to help you improve your grades and exam performance. With a wide range of tutors to choose from and flexible scheduling options, we make it easy to get the help you need to succeed in A-Level Chemistry and other subjects.

A-Levels can be considered challenging due to their academic rigour and high standards of achievement. In 2021, over 305,000 students in the UK sat A-Level exams across a range of subjects, making them one of the most popular and competitive qualifications available to high school students. The pass rate for A-Levels was 99.7%, with 44.8% of students achieving an A* or A grade. While the number of A* grades has increased in recent years, competition for places at top universities remains fierce. With careful preparation and support, however, many students are able to succeed and achieve the grades they need to progress to their desired university course or career.

At Beyond Tutors, we provide A-Level tutoring for all exam boards, including AQA, OCR, Edexcel, WJEC, CCEA, and Cambridge International. Each exam board has its own syllabus and assessment style, and our tutors are experienced in delivering lessons that align with each exam board's requirements. <br> <br> The content of each exam board's A-Level courses will differ slightly, with variations in the topics covered and the level of detail required. For example, AQA and OCR are known for their more in-depth approach, while Edexcel tends to cover a broader range of topics. Cambridge International A-Levels are designed to be more flexible and have a broader range of subject choices compared to other exam boards.<br> <br> Understanding these differences is important when choosing an exam board, as it can impact which university courses a student is eligible to apply for. Our tutors are well-versed in the differences between exam boards and can help students choose the one that is best suited to their goals and aspirations.
