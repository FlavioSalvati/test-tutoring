To convert $4500$ millilitres to litres, you simply divide $4500$ by $1000$, resulting in $4.5$ litres.

Understanding the relationship between millilitres and litres is essential, as both units are part of the metric system, which is widely used in the UK and based on powers of ten. This system facilitates straightforward conversions. Specifically, one litre is equal to $1000$ millilitres. Thus, to convert millilitres to litres, you divide the number of millilitres by $1000$.

For instance, to convert $4500$ millilitres to litres, you perform the following calculation:

$$ 
4500 \div 1000 = 4.5 
$$

This calculation shows that $4500$ millilitres is equivalent to $4.5$ litres.

Understanding this conversion can be quite beneficial in various real-life scenarios, such as measuring liquids for recipes, conducting scientific experiments, or topping up a vehicle with petrol. Keeping in mind that $1000$ millilitres make up $1$ litre can help you quickly and accurately convert between these two units.
